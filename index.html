Why Choose Akroyd's

Everything Your Vehicle Deserves

Over 30 years of trusted, family-run storage — with some customers who've been with us for 25 years. Here's what makes Akroyd's different.

CCTV Surveillance

Cameras cover the entire site around the clock — day, night, weekends and bank holidays. Your vehicle is always under watchful eyes.

Palisade Security Fencing

Heavy-duty palisade security fencing runs around the entire perimeter, providing a robust physical barrier against unauthorised access.

Extra Wide 3.2m Pitches

Our pitches are an extra-wide 3.2 metres — giving you plenty of room to manoeuvre even the largest caravans and motorhomes with ease.

All Hardstanding Pitches

Every single pitch on our site is hardstanding — no muddy ground, no rocking, no damage to your vehicle's chassis or tyres.

Site Security

Your Caravan's Safety Is Our Priority

After over 30 years in the business, we know what caravan and motorhome owners need most: genuine peace of mind. Here's how we deliver it.

CCTV Surveillance

Cameras cover all areas of the site 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — including overnight, weekends and bank holidays.
